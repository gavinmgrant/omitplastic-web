## üç± Swap #6: Glass or Stainless Steel Food Storage

Plastic containers can leach chemicals into food, especially when heated. Glass and stainless steel containers are safer, more durable, and completely plastic-free.

**Why this swap matters:**
- No chemical leaching concerns
- Can go from freezer to oven safely
- Lasts for decades
- Better at preserving food freshness

**What to look for:**
- Glass containers with silicone lids (not plastic)
- Stainless steel bento boxes
- Mason jars for bulk storage
- Silicone storage bags (reusable alternative to Ziploc)

## üß¥ Swap #7: Refillable Cleaning Products

**Why this swap matters:**
- Reduces plastic bottle waste dramatically
- Often more concentrated and cost-effective
- Many refill stations now available
- Solid options eliminate packaging completely

**Options to consider:**
- Refillable cleaning spray systems
- Solid dish soap bars
- Laundry detergent sheets or pods in cardboard
- Concentrated cleaners you dilute at home
